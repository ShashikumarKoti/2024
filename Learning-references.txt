Isolation:
1.Repeatable-Read:
	Data modified in txn1 will not be visible to txn2 until the txn2 commits or comes out of the transaction.
	
2.Read-Uncommmitted:
	Data modified in txn1 and which is not yet committed. The same modified data is visible to txn2...which leads to data inconsistency.
	
3.Read-committed:
	Data modified in txn1 and which is Committed. Only the committed data is visible in txn2.
